How to Use the Font Accessibility Checker

1. **Select a Font:** Choose your desired font family from the dropdown list.
2. **Enter Sample Text:** Type or paste text into the sample text area.
3. **Click Analyze:** Hit the "Analyze Accessibility" button to get recommendations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What accessibility standards are used?
A: This tool primarily uses guidelines from WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) 2.1 and 2.2 for font-related recommendations.

Q: Does this check color contrast?
A: Basic contrast notes are provided, but for a comprehensive color contrast check, please use our Font Color Tester tool.

Q: Can I simulate visual impairments?
A: Advanced simulation features are planned for future updates.
