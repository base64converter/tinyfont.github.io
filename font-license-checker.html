Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are all Google Fonts free to use?
A: Yes, all fonts on Google Fonts are open source and free for both personal and commercial use. However, specific licenses may have minor differences in terms of modification and redistribution.

Q: What is the difference between OFL and Apache licenses?
A: The Open Font License (OFL) is specifically designed for fonts, allowing free use, modification, and distribution. The Apache License 2.0 is a permissive free software license. Both generally allow commercial use and modification.

Q: Can I embed these fonts on my website?
A: Yes, all Google Fonts are designed to be easily embedded on websites.
